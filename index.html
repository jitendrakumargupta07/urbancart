FAQ

Everything you need to know about shopping with UrbanCart

Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days across India. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available for major metros including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Pune.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹79 applies.
Can I track my order?
Absolutely. Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also track your order from your UrbanCart account dashboard.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Simply initiate a return from your account or contact our support team.
How long does it take to get a refund?
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. For UPI and wallet payments, refunds typically appear faster than bank transfers.
Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes! Size exchanges are free of charge. Simply initiate a return and place a new order, or contact our support team who will handle the exchange directly for you.

Sizing & Products

How do I find my correct size?
Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements in centimetres. We recommend measuring your chest, waist, and hips and comparing with our size chart. When in doubt between two sizes, we recommend sizing up for outerwear and sizing true for knitwear.
Are your products ethically made?
Yes. All our manufacturing partners are audited for fair wages, safe working conditions, and environmental compliance. We're committed to transparent and ethical supply chains across India and South Asia.
How do I care for my UrbanCart garments?
Care instructions are printed on every garment label. As a general rule, we recommend cold machine wash or hand wash, avoiding tumble drying for delicate items. Our knitwear should be laid flat to dry to maintain shape.
